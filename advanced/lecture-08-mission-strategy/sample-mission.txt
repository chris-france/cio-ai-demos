MERIDIAN HEALTH SYSTEMS — CORPORATE MISSION STATEMENT

Mission:
Meridian Health Systems exists to deliver exceptional, patient-centered healthcare
that is accessible, affordable, and innovative. We leverage cutting-edge technology
and evidence-based practices to improve health outcomes across every community we serve.

Vision:
To be the most trusted healthcare network in the region by 2030, recognized for
clinical excellence, digital innovation, and equitable access to care.

Core Values:
1. Patient First — Every decision starts with the patient experience
2. Innovation — We embrace technology that improves outcomes and reduces cost
3. Integrity — Transparent operations, ethical practices, honest communication
4. Collaboration — Cross-functional teams, shared knowledge, no silos
5. Equity — Equal access to quality care regardless of background or geography

Strategic Priorities (Board-Approved, FY2026):
- ACCELERATE digital transformation across all 12 hospital campuses
- REDUCE operational costs by 15% through automation and process improvement
- EXPAND telehealth services to reach 50,000 additional rural patients
- STRENGTHEN cybersecurity posture to meet evolving HIPAA requirements
- ATTRACT and retain top clinical and technical talent in a competitive market
- INTEGRATE AI-assisted diagnostics into radiology, pathology, and emergency triage
- MODERNIZE legacy EHR systems to improve clinician workflow and reduce burnout

Annual Revenue: $2.8B
IT Budget: $112M (4% of revenue)
Employees: 18,500
Locations: 12 hospitals, 45 outpatient clinics, 3 data centers
Current IT Staff: 340 FTEs
